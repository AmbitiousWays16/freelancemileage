# MileTrack Quick Start Guide

A quick reference for getting started with MileTrack as quickly as possible.

## üöÄ For New Users

### 1. Access the App
- Visit the MileTrack application URL
- Click "Sign in with Google"
- Authorize the application

### 2. Complete Your Profile
After first login, you'll be prompted to:
- Enter your first and last name
- Specify your business name
- Choose your business type (Rideshare, Delivery, Service Provider, etc.)
- Add your phone number
- Set your home address
- (Optional) Add tax ID for professional invoices

### 3. Log Your First Trip

**Quick Method:**
1. Today's date is pre-selected
2. "From" field auto-fills with your home address
3. Enter destination address (or select a saved client)
4. Select/add client and project
5. Enter business purpose
6. Click "Calculate Route"
7. Review the map and mileage
8. Click "Add Trip"

**What you'll see:**
- Exact mileage calculated by Google Maps
- Visual route map
- Estimated tax deduction (based on IRS rate)

### 4. Export Your Records

**CSV Export (Free):**
- Click the "Export" button
- Choose month or custom date range
- Download opens automatically
- Import into Excel/Sheets

**PDF Export (Premium):**
- Professional report with route maps
- Business branding included
- Tax-ready format
- Perfect for accountants

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What mileage rate does MileTrack use?**  
A: We use the current IRS standard mileage rate ($0.70/mile for 2025).

**Q: Can I edit or delete trips?**  
A: Yes! Click the edit or trash icon on any trip.

**Q: What happens to archived trips?**  
A: They're moved to a separate view but remain accessible for reports.

**Q: Can I use MileTrack for personal miles?**  
A: MileTrack is designed for business mileage only. Personal miles shouldn't be logged.

**Q: How accurate is the mileage calculation?**  
A: We use Google Maps Directions API, which provides highly accurate driving distances.

**Q: Can I download my data?**  
A: Yes! Use the CSV export to get all your trip data in a spreadsheet format.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**  
A: The web app is mobile-optimized and works great in mobile browsers. Native apps are planned.

**Q: What if the route isn't accurate?**  
A: You can manually edit the mileage after calculation if needed.
